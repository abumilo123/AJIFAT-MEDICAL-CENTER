Our Mission & Vision

AJIFAT MEDICAL CENTER is dedicated to providing accessible, affordable and quality healthcare services to the people of Ilorin and surrounding communities. Our mission is to save lives through compassionate, professional medical care delivered with integrity and accountability.

We envision becoming the most trusted healthcare destination in Kwara State, known for emergency excellence, maternal care leadership and diagnostic precision.
